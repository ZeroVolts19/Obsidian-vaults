**There is no situation where you should leave your red lock on a piece of equipment and go home.** **There are BLUE out of service locks for a piece of equipment that is not ready to return to service but you yourself are no longer working on it for the day.**

Red Locks - Personal, assigner per TM
Yellow Locks - Group lockout: Used for locking energy isolation or control devices 
Green - Group lockout: team lead lock
Blue Locks - Out of service, need to list reason for lockout on the tag.

Sign in, and add lock to group lockout box before entering Danger Zone, Remove lock and sign out when work is completed.
