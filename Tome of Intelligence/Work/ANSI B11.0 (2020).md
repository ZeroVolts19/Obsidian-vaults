## Foundations:
**Documentation**- is essential to keeping the decision making about acceptable risk a rational and traceable process.
**Hazard** - physical origin of harm (I.e a rotating shaft).
**Risk** - a combination of how bad it could be if a person was exposed, and how likely it is to happen.
	- Composed of the two-factors of a risk assessment: **Severity** and **Probability**. 
		- **Probability** is composed of **Frequency** of occurrence (how often & for how long) and, **Possibility** of avoidance.
