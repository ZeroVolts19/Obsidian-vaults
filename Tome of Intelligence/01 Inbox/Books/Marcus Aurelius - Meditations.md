- **Zettel:** Mini Dialogue: 
   Neo: Tell me, what is your craft? 
   Man: To be good. 
   Neo: Well then, how do you practice It? 
   Man: I endevor to know my own Way, and to know, or rather I cannot fully know the Ways of Nature; But mainly, by benefitting others. 
   Neo: Should one try to be good?
   Man: Do good within your abilities, But don't hunt a false superiority through acts. One should strive to normal not special.
