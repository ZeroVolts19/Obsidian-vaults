HTRAB rule set #1:
1. **Classify the book** by the kind of book it is and the subject matter it has. 
	1. Tips, review: title, subtitle, preface, ToC, opening sentences.
2. **Summarize** the entire book as briefly as possible in your own words.
3. **Outline/see/know** the books **major parts** and their **order**.
4. Define the **problems** the **author is** trying **to solve**.
HTRAB rule set #2:
5. Come to **terms** with an author. Understand what **his basic words** mean when he uses them. 
6. **Find** and state the **authors major propositions.** (Highlight pink)
7. Detail which of the authors original **problems he actually solved in the book** and which did he not solve.
HTRAB rule set #3:


HTRAB questions:

What is the book about as a whole? how are its parts related to that set of ideas? What is the unity of the book? 


What in detail does the book say? What does the author mean by what he says? What are the main propositions? 

Is it true? If so, what of it?
